About Us

Lyons Family Tree Care is a second-generation, locally owned and operated tree service company proudly serving Polk County, Florida, and the surrounding areas. What began as a small tree trimming business over 25 years ago has since grown into one of Central Florida's most trusted names in professional tree care. Our commitment to quality service, safety, and customer satisfaction has helped us build lasting relationships with our community. Whether you need tree trimming, removal, or emergency services, we are here to provide reliable, expert care for your trees.

Certified Arborists & Tree Experts

We employ only certified arborists who have passed rigorous exams covering all aspects of arboriculture. These professionals possess a deep understanding of tree biology, growth requirements, and maintenance techniques, ensuring the long-term health and structural integrity of your trees.

Whether it's a challenging tree removal or precise, artistic pruning, you can trust our crews to handle each job safely and skillfully. We are committed to staying ahead of the curve by investing in the latest safety practices, state-of-the-art equipment, and advanced technology, ensuring that we deliver consistently outstanding results for every project.
